e-Portfolio: Machine Learning

A collection of my data science projects and my reflections on the subject matter.

Air BNB Exploratory Data Analysis

For this assignment the team was tasked with advising AirBnB executives on the future direction of AirBnB by looking at the current data of listings in New York. Using the AirBnB dataset, we had to answer a question on weather customers in New York have distinct features they look for when making premium bookings. By understanding these premium services, AirBNB marketing team can tailor their premium service offerings to attract and maintain high value clientele.

Wine Quality Exploratory Data Analysis

Using Wine Quality dataset taken off Kaggle,to perform Exploratory data analysis and present statistical summaries.

Insurance Data: Logistic Regression

In this exercise, the task is to estimate at what age clients are likely to purchase insurance. Using insurance data that shows the age of clients and whether or not they have insurance, I created a learning model using the scikit library to create a model that accurately predict the age when a client will likely agree to purchase insurance.

K Means Clustering: AirBnB New York Listings

This exercise continues from the previous AirBnB analysis. After identifying the top three boroughs with the most listings, we employed K-means clustering, an unsupervised learning method, to group similar data points. The objective was to highlight the top AirBnB listings in Brooklyn, Queens, and Manhattan.
